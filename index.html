01 — About
Soldier. Peacekeeper.
IT Enthusiast.

My career started in uniform — 14 years in the Serbian Army, including a 7-month deployment with the UN peacekeeping mission MINUSCA in the Central African Republic. Operational work far from home, in conditions that demand precision and a cool head.

Then came IT. Network infrastructure, security, automations, compliance. ISO standards, BCP plans, policies, training. Everything that keeps a modern company operational and secure.

Outside of work — a macro lens pointed at a dewdrop, a digital telescope aimed at M42, a fly rod above the Drina, or an hour spent watching the reef tank.
